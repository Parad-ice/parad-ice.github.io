In a world where people are getting more health-conscious, vegan ice cream is no longer a strange thing to hear. But here at Parad’ice, we are devoted to taking the vegan lifestyle commitment one step further: by creating the finest blend of local Indonesian flavors with our oat + coconut-based ice cream.

Zesty Twist

Sweet, sour, and citrusy, the Zesty Twist finds the right balance between that sharp key lime taste and the sugary sensation ice cream fans love. Sprinkled with exclusive lime fragrance, this ice cream will remind you of summer beaches and cold juice on a sunny day.

Coffee Affair

Coffee ice cream is no substitute for the drink, but Coffee Affair delivers that smoky smell reminiscent of coffee houses and café hangouts. Cold, sweet, and bitter, just like any whirlwind holiday romance, this robusta-powdered ice cream promises the adventure you need for your frozen coffee vacation.

Cincau Delight

Soft by the first bite and lingeringly sweet by the second, Cincau Delight offers the tropical holiday breeze everyone needs on their dream vacation. The syrupy brown sugar-infused base, complemented with fresh grass jellies, will leave your tongue with a taste impossible to satiate without eating more.
